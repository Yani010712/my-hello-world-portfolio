About me.

Yani Rodriguez

I was born and raised in Florida, not in the state of Florida, but a small town in Cuba with the same name. I do not remember having passion for any profession when I was younger so when I finished high school I entered the University of Computer Science of Havana only for the wish to go to university, but in reality, I had never had a computer in my life, so my knowledge of computing was vary basic at the beginning.

I graduated without obstacles, but without a lot of enthusiasm. Two years later, I was lucky enough to emigrate to the United States, where for reasons that no one knows I started working as a TV producer. In the beginning, it was enjoyable, but after a while, I began to miss working in technology and resigned that job.

Was then when I realized that it had been five years and the world of technology moved very fast, and I had stayed behind, it was then when I began to research how I could get back on track with technology and discovered Wyncode, a coding Bootcamp. For the first time in my life, I feel that I found my true passion, and I am pleased about that.

Now that I love being a software engineer, I am very interested in using innovative technology to solve educational, social, and environmental problems. More about me.

I love coffee, to build origami, and play Sudoku.
